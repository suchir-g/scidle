SCIDLE

If you don't know how to play the original WORDLE, check that out before playing this game.


A green tile means that the tile is in the word and in the right position.

An example of this would be He as it is in the word, and in the right place.


A yellow tile means that the tile is in the word but not in the right place.

An example of this would be Er as it is in the word, but in the last position instead of the 2nd.


A blue tile means that the tile is not in the word, but a similar tile is.

An example of this would be Pa, as it isn't in the word but Pt is, and they are very close


A grey tile means that the tile is not in the word at all, and nothing similar is in the tile as well.

For example Ti isn't in the word, and nothing similar is in the word. This means that for example Ta wouldn't be in the word as it is similar to Ti.
